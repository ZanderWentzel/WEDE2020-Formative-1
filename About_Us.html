About Us

Get to know the heart of Trooper Fitness

Our Story

Trooper Fitness was founded with a single goal: to create a space where fitness meets lifestyle. Based in Hartbeespoortdam, South Africa, our community thrives on functional training, supportive coaching, and a culture that makes fitness enjoyable and sustainable.

Our Mission

We strive to inspire individuals to reach their fullest potential through inclusive fitness programs, nutritional support, and a motivating community. Fitness is more than exercise – it’s about confidence, balance, and long-term health.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert coaches dedicated to your progress
  • A supportive, family-like community
  • Diverse classes for all levels
  • Holistic approach: fitness + nutrition
